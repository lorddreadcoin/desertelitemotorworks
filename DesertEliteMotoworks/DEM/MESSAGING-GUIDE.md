## For High-End Clients (Your New Target)

### When They Call:
**Opening**: "Thank you for calling Desert Elite Motorworks. This is [Name]. How may I assist you with your vehicle today?"

**NOT**: "Yeah, Nick's shop, what's up?"

### When They Visit:
**Greeting**: "Welcome to Desert Elite Motorworks. I'm [Name]. What brings you in today?"

**Offer**: "Can I get you a water or coffee while we discuss your vehicle?"

### During Service Discussion:
**Professional**:
- "I'll need to perform a comprehensive diagnostic..."
- "We use only OEM or premium aftermarket parts..."
- "I'll provide you with a detailed estimate..."
- "We can arrange pickup and delivery at your convenience..."

**NOT**:
- "Yeah, I can fix that..."
- "I got some parts in the back..."
- "It'll probably cost around..."

### Pricing Conversations:
**Confidence**: 
- "The investment for this service is $X,XXX"
- "This includes our lifetime warranty and white-glove service"
- "I stand behind this work completely"

**NEVER**:
- "I mean, I guess it could be around..."
- "If that's too much..."
- "I could do it cheaper if..."

## For Classic Car Collectors

### Initial Contact:
"I understand you have a [year/make/model]. That's a special vehicle. Tell me about your restoration goals."

### Key Points to Hit:
- Period-correct approach
- Documentation of work
- Source for original parts
- Experience with similar builds
- Timeline and milestones

### Language They Respect:
- "Concours-level restoration"
- "Original specifications"
- "Documented history"
- "Matching numbers"
- "Investment grade"

### What NOT to Say:
- "I'll just throw some parts on it"
- "Good enough"
- "Close enough"

---

## For Exotic/Luxury Owners

### They Care About:
1. Expertise with their specific brand
2. No damage to their vehicle
3. Convenience
4. Discretion
5. Communication

### Opening Statement:
"I specialize in [European/exotic/luxury] vehicles. I've worked extensively with [their brand]. Walk me through what you're experiencing with your vehicle."

### During Service:
- Send photos with updates
- Text, don't just call
- Be proactive about delays
- Explain the "why" not just "what"

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- "I've never worked on one of these but..."
- "This is interesting, let me figure it out..."
- Radio silence for days

---

## For Regular/Returning Clients

### Balancing Acts:
They built your reputation. Don't abandon them. But set boundaries.

**The Message**:
"We're elevating our service to match the quality you've always received. New facility, same trusted expertise. You're family - we're just making sure you get treated like it."

### Pricing Evolution:
"Our pricing reflects the premium service and facility we're now providing. However, as a valued long-term client, [loyalty consideration if applicable]."

### Don't:
- Make them feel "not good enough"
- Apologize for success
- Completely change pricing overnight

## Phone Script Template

**Greeting**:
"Good [morning/afternoon], Desert Elite Motorworks, this is [Name]. How may I help you today?"

**Capture Info**:
"May I have your name and the best number to reach you?"
"What type of vehicle do you have?" [Year, Make, Model]
"What service are you looking for today?"

**Set Expectations**:
"I'd like to schedule a consultation where we can see your vehicle in person and provide you with a detailed estimate. Do you prefer [day] at [time] or [day] at [time]?"

**Close**:
"Perfect, I have you scheduled for [date/time]. I'll send you a confirmation text/email with directions to our facility. Is there anything else I can help you with today?"

**If They Ask Price Over Phone**:
"I want to give you accurate pricing, which requires seeing the vehicle. Our consultations are complimentary, and I can have a detailed estimate for you within 30 minutes of seeing your vehicle. Would [day/time] work for your schedule?"

## Objection Handling

### "That's Too Expensive"

**Response**: 
"I understand your concern. Let me break down what you're getting: [list value]. We're not the cheapest option, but we are the best investment in your vehicle's longevity and your peace of mind. Many of our clients chose us after experiencing issues with lower-cost providers."

### "Can You Match [Competitor] Price?"

**Response**:
"We price based on the quality of our work, the parts we use, and the service we provide. I'm confident that once you experience our white-glove service and see the quality of our work, you'll understand the value. That said, I'm happy to review the estimate with you to ensure you understand exactly what you're getting."

### "I Need to Think About It"

**Response**:
"Absolutely, this is an important decision. What specific concerns do you have? Is it the timeline, the investment, or something else? I want to make sure you have all the information you need."

### "My Buddy Can Do It Cheaper"

**Response**:
"That's great that you have options. We specialize in [specific service] with [specific expertise]. If your friend can deliver the same quality and service, that may be a good option for you. However, if you want [benefit], we're here for you. Many of our clients come to us after having issues elsewhere."

## The Golden Rules

1. **Always be professional, never stuffy**
2. **Confident, not cocky**
3. **Solution-focused, not problem-focused**
4. **Under-promise, over-deliver**
5. **Every interaction is a brand experience**
6. **Your reputation is your currency**
7. **Treat every vehicle like it's priceless**
8. **Communicate proactively**
9. **Own mistakes immediately**
10. **Always be the expert in the room**
